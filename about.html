CineSeeker internship project

What CineSeeker includes

TMDB-powered trending, popular, top rated, and upcoming sections
Movie details with cast, trailer, recommendations, and production info
Local storage favorites, recently viewed items, and theme persistence
Responsive glassmorphism UI with skeleton loaders and accessibility support
